# Ethical Governance: Responsible AI Use Guidelines

This guidelines matrix dictates how the customer retention team should and should not act on the outputs generated by the churn prediction microservice.

---

## ✅ Permitted Strategic Uses (The Green Zone)
* **Proactive Outreach Support:** Prioritizing service queues to route customers facing high support friction directly to specialized support managers.
* **Milestone Incentives:** Awarding high-margin subscription discounts, extended account configuration support, or loyalty perks to users flagged as high risk.
* **User Experience Improvement:** Using aggregated risk signals to identify and optimize bug-heavy features or friction points inside the web app.

## ❌ Forbidden Action Matrix (The Red Zone)
* **Punitive Pricing Strategies:** Increasing subscription fees, adding account access restrictions, or lowering service quality levels for users flagged as likely to leave.
* **Aggressive Communication:** Sending automated marketing emails multiple times a day, which can annoy customers and accelerate their departure.
* **Demographic Discrimination:** Using prediction signals to offer preferential treatment based on protected demographic indicators, ensuring entry-level customer tiers are not systematically marginalized.
